PECAN CHICKEN SALAD

How To Make Peacan Chicken Salad

This pecan chicken salad made with chopped cooked chicken, herbs, celery, pecans, red onion, and a creamy homemade dressing is really flavorful. Serve on top of toasted croissants or butter lettuce.

Peacan Chicken Salad Ingredients

The Allrecipes community adores this Pecan Chicken Salad recipe because it's incredibly customizable, so you can easily alter the ingredient list to suit your needs. If you want to stay true to the original recipe, though, these are the ingredients you'll need to add to your grocery list:

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yoghurt
  • 2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon Park Hill maple pepper
  • 2 cups chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 stalks celery, diced
  • 1/3 cup chopped toasted pecans
  • 2 tablespoons minced red onion
